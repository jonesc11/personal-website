How'd I start?

In sixth grade, I starting playing with extremely basic HTML. At first, I just enjoyed making a bunch of links talk to each other.

As middle school progressed, I started to work on more complicated websites. One of my friends asked me to make a website for his gaming server, and this was my first real project. In this project, I played with many, many more CSS styling options, and started making websites that not only functioned, but also looked good.

Since then, I started school at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, and I'm now studying Web Science as one of my majors, and I've had multiple projects since then...
