Welcome!

This is RPI FiXXer, the web application companion for Fixx.

The Facilities Customer Service Center is the central clearinghouse for the maintenance needs of residents.
Please use this platform to communicate all your requests. Submitting a request will enter the job directly into our computer database and assign a maintenance crew whose goal is to answer all request in 48 hours. If a repair cannot be made within 48 hours, students will be notified with further information and instructions.

If you have any further questions or concerns please contact us at 276-2000 or email fixx@rpi.edu
